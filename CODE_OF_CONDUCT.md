\# Code of Conduct



\## Our Pledge



We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and welcoming experience for everyone.



\## Expected Behavior



\- Be respectful.

\- Be constructive.

\- Help others learn.

\- Keep discussions professional.



\## Unacceptable Behavior



\- Harassment

\- Personal attacks

\- Spam

\- Offensive language



\## Enforcement



Project maintainers reserve the right to remove inappropriate content or restrict participation when necessary.
